Colorado County Sheriff's Office
Attn: Custodian of Records
2215 Walnut St, Columbus, TX 78934

**Re: Public Information Request — Personnel Records, Lt. Holly Smith and Jailer Perla Flores**

Pursuant to the Texas Public Information Act, Tex. Gov't Code Chapter 552, I hereby request the following public records for **Lieutenant Holly Smith** and **Jailer Perla Flores**, both employees of the Colorado County Sheriff's Office:

1. Job title, classification, and employment dates
2. Current and historical salary, compensation, and pay grade
3. All formal disciplinary records, reprimands, suspensions, or termination proceedings
4. All use-of-force reports authored by or involving either officer
5. Any internal affairs or misconduct investigations

I am **not** requesting and ask that the following be redacted if incidentally present: date of birth, Social Security number, home address, personal email address, and personally owned telephone numbers.

I am specifically **not** requesting medical records, personal financial information, or home contact information, as I recognize those may be protected under § 552.102 or § 552.117.

Pursuant to § 552.267, I request a waiver of all fees associated with this request. Disclosure of these records primarily benefits the general public by promoting transparency and accountability in the operations of a county law enforcement agency, and not a private commercial interest.

Please provide records in electronic format. If any portion is withheld, please submit to the Attorney General for a ruling pursuant to § 552.301.

Sincerely,

██████████████████
