🚨Update on the plight of Hays County Judge Becerra🚨 Judge Becerra lost his appeal to the State Commission On Judicial Conduct (SCJC) and his public admonishment has been upheld. This matters to us because Lavaca County Judge Keith Mudd has done something seemingly very similar by having his private company website address on his public Judge Facebook page. Mudd claims it’s a “campaign” page though the profile picture shows him in his robe, holding a gavel, sitting on the Bench, and his cover photo is the courthouse. I have filed a formal complaint with the SCJC. I’m very curious to see what they do. And of course ask “why”. Stay tuned #Corruption #watch #texas
